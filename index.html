Our Story

How we came to existence

  • It Begins At a Festival

    The first year we went to a festival it was magical, and we've never seen anything like it. Watching all the people dress up and get into it gave a whole new meaning.

  • Along Comes a Totem

    Among all the things you encounter at a festival, there's nothing as fun as totems. Totems are a beacon of energy and excitement, each bringing their own flavor to the crowd.

  • The Stuggle is Real

    After spending time working on our own totems, we learned they can be a constant headache. Most totems fall apart or are smashed and usually require a good amount of time and money to upkeep.

  • A Light At the End

    We decided it's time to create a solution that can be easily attained and withstand the harshest of climates. All the while giving you a totem that Lights up and keeps the place energized.

  • Now
    Here
    We Are!
